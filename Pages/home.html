What is IT?

Information Technology (IT) is the use of technology to manage, store, retrieve, and transmit data. It encompasses various fields like programming, networking, database management, and software development to solve real-world problems.

What is a Front-End Developer?

A front-end developer focuses on the visual elements of a website or application, ensuring it is user-friendly and visually appealing. Using tools like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and prototyping platforms like Figma, they bridge the gap between design and functionality.
